Our pizza recipes :


Crab pizza :

  1. Spread the tomato sauce or fresh cream on the pizza dough, then add the crab crumbs and small shrimp.
  2. Cut the mozzarella and the tomato into thin slices (1 cm, no more). Place a slice of mozzarella on the crab and shrimp, then a slice of tomato (so on).
  3. Then add on top if you want a little grated cheese.
  4. Put in the oven for about 15 minutes at a thermostat of about 180°C.









Seafood pizza :

  1. Roll out the pizza dough with a rolling pin into a regular disc of the desired diameter.
  2. Arrange the disc of dough on a pizza grid with a slightly larger diameter.
  3. In a non-stick skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  4. Add the finely minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  5. Add the peeled mussels, the minced calamari and the peeled pink shrimp. Continue cooking for 2 to 3 minutes. Season with fine salt and
  6. Let cool slightly and spread the seafood on the disc of raw dough.
  7. Add 3 to 4 hazelnuts of heavy cream.
  8. Sprinkle with dried oregano.
  9. Then bake in a ventilated oven, preheated to 240°C, halfway up and for about ten minutes until the dough is golden brown.



Salmon pizza :

  1. Prepare the pizza dough. Wait at least 15 min for it to rise, it will be easier to work with.
  2. Spread the dough on a baking sheet greased with olive oil, then cover it with a thin layer of fresh cream.
  3. Arrange the salmon slices so that they cover the entire dough.
  4. Drizzle with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, sprinkle with oregano and some lemon zest.
  5. Finally, cut the mozzarella into medium slices to cover the pizza; you can garnish with a few slices of lemon.
  6. Bake in a preheated oven at 180°C (th 6).
  7. Optional: crack the eggs 5 minutes before the end of cooking and return to the oven for a few minutes.







pizza fisherman :

  1. Roll out the pizza dough on the baking sheet, spread the tomato sauce on it and sprinkle with pizza spices.
  2. Arrange the finely chopped half onion and the previously crumbled tuna in a bowl on the pizza.
  3. Pat lightly on the tuna so that it soaks up the tomato sauce so that it does not dry out during cooking.
  4. Divide the detailed olives into small pieces, then 6 to 7 tablespoons of liquid cream. Salt, pepper.
  5. Arrange the mozzarella cut into small cubes.
  6. Put in the oven and cook at 180° for 30 minutes.
